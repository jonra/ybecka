We are inspired by and believe in the potential of young people.

 
 
 

Imagine a future where an entrepreneurial mindset is cultivated and nurtured in our youth. A future where young people gain the right set of skills to drive their own destiny. This is the future Y-BECA is building.

Y-BECA is a non-profit organization that engages vulnerable 15-25yr old unemployed & out-of-school youth from under-resourced communities in experiential entrepreneurship education.

Our Mission

Our mission is to impact society and address the economic challenges of at-risk youth by encouraging and teaching them to be innovative and entrepreneurial. In so doing, our aim is to get them back into the labour market where they can potentially create jobs as well.

Our Approach

An intensive and experiential 12 week program

 
 

Get Involved and Support Us

 
 
 

Funds from Corporate partners, Governments, NGOs and Individuals allow us to expand our reach and offer our entrepreneurship programme to more unemployed youth in more communities and countries. Get involved and support us if you believe, as we do, in a future where young people have the mindset and skill set to identify and pursue opportunities & create jobs in their communities.
