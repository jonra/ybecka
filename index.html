Imagine a future where an entrepreneurial mindset is cultivated & nurtured from an early age.

A future where young people gain the right set of skills to drive their own destiny. This is the future Y-Beca is building. Y-Beca is a non-profit organization that engages high-school students & vulnerable out-of-school youth in experiential entrepreneurship education. We are inspired by and believe in the immense potential of young people. .

Our Mission

 
 
 

Our mission is to impact society and address economic challenges of young people by educating and empowering them to drive their own economic success.

We envision a future where young people create their own opportunities through enterprise building and thereby impact the economies of their own communities and the world.

What We DO

 
 
 

Masterclasses

We deliver experiential entrepreneurship & innovation masterclasses for two target groups: high school teenagers & vulnerable out-of-school youth. Our sessions are designed to allow young people to apply new skills, innovate and build a business in a supportive and collaborative setting. Our programme is delivered at schools and community settings.

Incubator Programme

For student and out-of-school youth businesses, we provide continuous business advice, mentorship and micro-finance for sound opportunities.

Entrepreneurship Champions

In order to expand the reach of our program, we train and provide tools to Volunteers seeking to deliver our program and mentor the youth in their communities.

Inspiration

We periodically select a few young people in our network to visit start-up festivals, participate in hackathons and maker-events, in their country.
