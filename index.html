Our Mission

 
 
 

Our mission is to impact society and address Youth unemployment in Africa & Europe through teaching Entrepreneurship Education to Teenagers in high school & out-of-school Youth.

We envision a future where young people create their own opportunities through enterprise building and thereby impacting their own Communities and the World.

Why People Like Us

 
 
 

Masterclasses

We’ve developed a framework-of-approach for teaching entrepreneurship & Innovation to young people. Our sessions are designed to allow young people to apply new skills in a supportive, collaborative and neutral setting.

Local Champions

We train local ‘entrepreneurship champions’ in all the countries where we give masterclasses. is allows for continuous embedding of our program and formation of local and accessible support.

Support & Advice

We give business & proposition advice to the youth in our program, as well as micro-nance for sound opportunities. Additionally, we conduct research on opportunities in local markets and share with our community of young people.

Network

We periodically select a few Young people in our network to visit startup festivals around the world for inspiration.
